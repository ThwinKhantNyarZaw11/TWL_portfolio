Trend Research & Mood Board

Project Overview

Conducted seasonal trend research and created mood boards to guide design direction for upcoming collections.

Key Features:

  • Monitored global fashion and jewelry trend reports
  • Collected visual inspiration from art, culture, and nature
  • Created digital and physical mood boards for team use
  • Aligned design choices with market preferences

Impact:

  • Improved collection cohesion and market relevance
  • Inspired innovative use of materials and forms
  • Supported creative direction in early planning stages
