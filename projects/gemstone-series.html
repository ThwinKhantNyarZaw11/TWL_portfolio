Gemstone Series Launch

Project Overview

Created a vibrant seasonal collection showcasing ethically sourced gemstones in contemporary designs.

Key Features:

  • Selected gemstones with fair-trade and ethical sourcing
  • Designed rings, earrings, and pendants with seasonal themes
  • Used vibrant color palettes to attract younger audiences
  • Promoted through pop-ups and online marketing

Impact:

  • Sold out 80% of collection within 2 weeks
  • Earned feature in a regional artisan showcase
  • Reinforced brand values in sustainability and design
